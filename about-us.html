JEVASOL Water Soluble Film

About company

Our priorities

Knowledge, commitment and communication are essential ingredients to innovation. JEVA has developed a diverse range of cold and hot water soluble PVA films to meet the specific industry requirements by listening to our clients. JEVA Packaging manufacturers water soluble PVA film (also known as PVOH) by a proprietary extrusion blown casting process, the most reliable of choice for the highest quality films without peer.

World fame

JEVASOL PVA films are known all over the world. These polyvinyl alcohol films produce and distribute custom designed and engineered for various industries and enable convenience and precision delivery. Detergents, agrochemicals, fertilizers and a variety other materials are delivered to the end users in an environmentally friendly way.

Experience and specialization

As an expert in the water-soluble packaging industry, JEVA can meet your needs by supplying innovative packaging solutions from soluble films to custom bags and by assisting you with new product development, trial runs and ongoing support.

Caring for the environment

JEVA Packaging We also produce water soluble packaging for light industries, chemicals, pesticides and medical applications. Our PVA products are environmentally friendly, non-toxic and 100% decomposed into water.

Benefits and safety

Our products are handled by people all over the world every day. From the detergent that you put in your dishwasher to the hospital laundry room. We make world-class quality products that are beneficial for mankind while also being safe for the environment.

Flexibility at work

JEVA Packaging team will help you choose or develop the right JEVASOL film to fit your demanding requirements. We will help you prioritize your requirements and design the best delivery system based on the following key properties:

  • Excellent film formation
  • Resistance
  • Water Solubility
  • Film Forming
  • Mechanical Properties
  • Delivery Mechanisms
  • Antistatic
  • Release Timing
  • Very good barrier properties
  • Biodegradability
