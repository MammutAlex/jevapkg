Chemical Packaging

JEVASOL PVA films with special water-soluble and biodegradable characteristics are widely used in the packaging of chemicals such as detergents, disinfectants, pesticides and dyes, dry ingredients, wettable powders, granules, gels and concentrated liquid active ingredients.

Chemicals used in agriculture, or in manufacturing, are usually very toxic, cause severe pollution and endanger our health. Therefore, more and more attention is paid to the safety of agricultural packaging materials. Although chemicals in conventional packaging have been used for a long time, they have two major drawbacks:
- the liquid chemical is packaged in glass bottles that break easily and cause a toxic chemical to leak out;
- A large amount of chemical residues in the package leads to the formation of a large amount of chemical waste that pollutes the soil and water, which in the long term is dangerous for the environment.

Delivering chemicals in JEVA Packaging's water-soluble film provides worker protection, ease of use, accurate dosing and safer, more efficient and environmentally friendly disposal of secondary containers.

The JEVA team understands the capabilities of the JEVASOL film and can determine whether a film class exists or can exist to meet design or performance criteria. After selecting the film, we can support your testing and packaging processes for chemicals to improve production technologies and equipment adapted to the properties of the film. Together we can turn ideas into practical applications that are uniquely found with water-soluble films.

Laundry Bags

JEVA Packaging is a leading developer and supplier of water-soluble packages for healthcare and infection control. JEVASOL laundry bags are designed to combat the risk of contamination and cross-contamination of medical personnel, including laundry personnel.

JEVA Packaging provides its own technological solution to these problems and difficulties.

The JEVASOL laundry bag is 100% soluble in the wash as soon as the set water temperature is reached, leaving no residue and effectively protecting personnel from the risk of contamination. These PVA packages are an anti-infection solution for sanitary and hygienic needs in hospitals and nursing homes, making it possible to wash dirty contaminated laundry in complete safety. Especially, such a solution is necessary in the departments for combating infectious diseases, which will allow workers to avoid direct contact with bacteria, pathogens and body fluids, preventing infection.

Laundry bags are anti-static, non-toxic, resistant to odors, resistant to gases and solvents and completely biodegradable (see here (hyperlink to)). JEVASOL PVA bags can dissolve, both in warm and hot water, without losing their strength and protection properties. During washing the package is dissolved and washed away into the sewage system, while not clogging the waste water with chemically harmful substances.

Transfer Printing

JEVA Packaging offers a unique technology of high-quality design of a wide range of forms and surfaces, be it from plastic, wood, ceramics, metal or composite materials. In this case, the size of the pattern can vary from applying to pens to applying to car parts. Due to the unique properties of JEVASOL PVO film, it will dissolve and be resistant to the application of different types of dyes on them; transfer of decoratively complex patterns to complex relief details has become accessible and simple. JEVASOL films are strong and durable, provide rich color reproduction, low pattern distortion and the possibility of multi-color printing, as well as provide easy three-dimensional printing. Hydrographic printing, also known as water transfer printing, is a unique process that adds visual appeal to any surfaces. The process begins with a drawing printed on a water-soluble JEVASOL film and after printing the desired pattern, three-dimensional objects can be immersed in a floating JEVASOL film with a pattern, so that the ink pattern is transferred to an object of complex shape, and the film dissolves.

JEVA Packaging helps you choose the color and formula of the dye.

JEVA Packaging experts offer a wide range of unique options for protective films to protect different surfaces: metal, plastic, glass surfaces, stainless steel surfaces, extrusion profiles, laminate, carpets, textiles, and even more.
