These films are environmentally friendly and fully biodegradable when disposed of in water or waste. JEVASOL films are an environmentally friendly solution that helps the consumer with accurate product dosage and safe handling/storage of toxic/hazardous products. Agro Chemical Film

Chemicals used in agriculture are usually very toxic, cause severe pollution and endanger our health. Therefore more and more attention have been paid to the safety of agricultural packaging materials. Although chemicals in conventional agricultural packaging have been used for a long time, they have two major drawbacks:
- a liquid agricultural chemical is packed in glass bottles that break easily and cause a toxic chemical to leak out;
- a large number of chemical residues in the package leads to the formation of a large amount of chemical waste that pollutes the soil and water, which in the long term is dangerous for the environment.

Delivering agrochemicals in water-soluble film by JEVA Packaging provides worker protection, convenience of use, precise dosing and safer, more efficient and environmentally-friendly secondary container disposal. JEVASOL soluble films are effective in packaging dry ingredients, wettable powders, granulars, gels and concentrated liquid active ingredients.

JEVA Packaging scientist's team understand the boundaries of JEVASOL film capabilities, and can determine if a film grade exists, or can exist to meet the design or performance criterion. Once a film is chosen, we can support your testing and processes in agrochemicals packaging, to perfect the production techniques and machinery tailored to the film properties. Together, we can turn ideas into practical applications uniquely met with water soluble films.

EVA Packaging are delivered agrochemicals and fertilizers to the end users in an environmentally friendly way and enable convenience and precision delivery. Visual instruction and more capabilities for use JEVASOL films in agrochemicals packaging you can find here

Embroidery and water transfer printing

The uniqueness of JEVASOL films allows you to create a wide range of solutions that simplify human life. Such are the decision to transfer embroidery or drawings made with various dyes to complex surfaces.

It is always difficult select and further utilizate of the bases for embroidery on light and translucent brittle fabrics, and JEVA Packaging offers a solution in the form of water-soluble durable films that are 100% recycled with cold or warm water without harming people and leaving no marks on the fabric.

Embroidery Film

JEVASOL PVO film is a cost-effective solution for customers in the field of embroidery, because it brings many additional functions to the embroidery process. Such films can be useful for a wide range of fabrics from thin, fragile, transparent and elastic, such as organza, tulle, silk, to fabrics, with short work, towels, knitwear, velvet and flocked fabrics, helping to avoid wrinkling and slipping of fabrics. and making it more sustainable. This will significantly speed up the process of applying the embroidery and save time. After the embroidery is applied, the film is easily removed by simply spraying with water, which is a unique solution for saving time and effort and remarkably maintains the quality of the embroidered design without damaging the fabric.

Depending on the requirements, JEVA Packaging will manufacture a water-soluble film in various sizes and thicknesses. In this way, the problem of the need for a more dense basis is solved in order that the above work is more sustainable.

Water Transfer Printing Film

JEVA Packaging offers a unique technology of high-quality design of a wide range of forms and surfaces, be it from plastic, wood, ceramics, metal or composite materials. In this case, the size of the pattern can vary from applying to pens to applying to car parts. JEVASOL films are strong and durable, provide rich color reproduction, low pattern distortion and the possibility of multi-color printing, as well as provide easy three-dimensional printing.

Hydrographic printing, also known as water transfer printing, is a unique process that adds visual appeal to any surfaces. The process begins with a drawing printed on a water-soluble JEVASOL film and after printing the desired pattern, three-dimensional objects can be immersed in a floating JEVASOL film with a pattern, so that the ink pattern is transferred to an object of complex shape, and the film dissolves.
