Detergent Packaging

The technology of water-soluble films changes the way of washing dishes.

Jevasol film is designed in such a way that it is compatible with liquid detergent for packaging, delivery, storage and use. JEVASOL water-soluble film technology allows us to pack concentrated detergents using fewer formulas to achieve the same high results.

You no longer need to look for special dishes to pack detergent or laundry powder, you no longer have to wear gloves and protect your breath. The pods not only increase convenience for the consumer, they also ensure correct dosing, safe handling and reduce the risk of spills.

Chemical Packaging

JEVASOL PVA films with special water-soluble and biodegradable characteristics are widely used in the packaging of chemicals such as detergents, disinfectants, pesticides and dyes, dry ingredients, wettable powders, granules, gels and concentrated liquid active ingredients.

Chemicals used in agriculture, or in manufacturing, are usually very toxic, cause severe pollution and endanger our health. Therefore, more and more attention is paid to the safety of agricultural packaging materials. Although chemicals in conventional packaging have been used for a long time, they have two major drawbacks: - the liquid chemical is packaged in glass bottles that break easily and cause a toxic chemical to leak out; - A large amount of chemical residues in the package leads to the formation of a large amount of chemical waste that pollutes the soil and water, which in the long term is dangerous for the environment.

The JEVA team understands the capabilities of the JEVASOL film and can determine whether a film class exists or can exist to meet design or performance criteria. After selecting the film, we can support your testing and packaging processes for chemicals to improve production technologies and equipment adapted to the properties of the film. Together we can turn ideas into practical applications that are uniquely found with water-soluble films.
