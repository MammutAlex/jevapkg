Laundry Bags

JEVA Packaging is a leading developer and supplier of water soluble bags for healthcare and infection control purposes. JEVASOL laundry bags are made to combat the risk of contamination and cross-infection of healthcare staff including laundry personnel. Once the linens are placed into our JEVASOL laundry bags, the contents of the bag are not touched till it is completely clean. The laundry bag dissolves in the wash leaving no residue and effectively protecting staff from the risk of infection. Our bags are anti-static, non-toxic, odor retentive, gas and solvent resistant and fully bio-degradable. JEVASOL PVA bags can be made warm or hot water soluble to handle moist linen.

Infection control is paramount in a medical environment. Whether handling linens, clothing, or instruments, it is vital to eliminate the risk of cross infection to staff, patients, and visitors. Jevasol provides clinical and technical use support worldwide in the containment area. Our range of fully-soluble laundry bags help to meet care guidelines by ensuring the safe isolation, transportation, and disinfection of soiled and comprised linens by conforming to HSG (95) 18 guidelines.
